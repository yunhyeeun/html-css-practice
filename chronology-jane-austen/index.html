Jane Austen

The writer who explored British landed gentry

Here's a time line of Jane Austen's life:

  • 1775 - Born in Steventon, Hampshire
  • 1795 - Writing "Elinor and Marianne" (which became Sense and Sensibility)
  • 1796 - Writing "First Impressions" (which became Pride and Prejudice)
  • 1798 - Writing Susan (which became Northanger Abbey)
  • 1811 - Sense and Sensibility published
  • 1811 - Writing Mansfield Park
  • 1813 - Pride and Prejudice published
  • 1814 - Mansfield Park published
  • 1814 - Writing Emma
  • 1815 - Writing Persuasion
  • 1816 - Emma published. Jane contracts her fatal illness
  • 1817 - dies
  • 1818 - Northange Abbey and Persuasion published together

"Of all great writers she is the most difficult to catch in the act of greatness."

-- Virginia Woolf
